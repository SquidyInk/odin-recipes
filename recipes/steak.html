Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • Butter for basting

Instructions

  1. Take the steaks out of the fridge and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  2. Season both sides of the steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until it is very hot.
  4. Add olive oil to the skillet and swirl to coat.
  5. Place the steaks in the skillet and sear for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  6. During the last minute of cooking, add minced garlic, rosemary, and a couple of tablespoons of butter to the skillet.
  7. Baste the steaks with the melted butter and herbs for extra flavor.
  8. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for about 5 minutes before slicing.
  9. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!

Notes

This steak recipe is perfect for a special occasion or a weekend dinner. The combination of garlic, rosemary, and butter adds a rich flavor to the juicy steaks.
