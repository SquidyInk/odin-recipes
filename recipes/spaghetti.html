Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain spaghetti and toss with the sauce.
  5. Serve with fresh basil leaves on top.

Notes

This is a simple and quick spaghetti recipe that can be made in under 30 minutes. Perfect for a weeknight dinner!
